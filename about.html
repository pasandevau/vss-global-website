Our Story

Founded in 2017 by Mr Thamara Wijesuriya, VSS Global started as a vision to bridge the gap between innovative technology and meaningful business impact.

In 2017, our CEO Mr Thamara Wijesuriya transformed his passion for digital innovation into VSS Global, initially focusing on web development and digital solutions for local businesses. Recognizing the growing demand for comprehensive digital transformation, Mr Wijesuriya expanded the company's vision to encompass the full spectrum of digital services.

Starting in Adelaide, VSS Global quickly broadened its reach, attracting clients across Australia and establishing strong partnerships with businesses in Sri Lanka. Our unique approach combines global best practices with local market understanding, enabling us to deliver solutions that resonate across diverse markets.

In 2020, we expanded our capabilities by establishing strategic partnerships with leading technology providers and investing heavily in emerging technologies like AI, machine learning, and cloud infrastructure. This expansion has not only broadened our service offerings but also deepened our commitment to creating digital experiences that drive sustainable business growth.

Our journey over the past 7+ years has earned us recognition as a trusted leader in digital innovation, with a growing portfolio of successful projects and long-term client partnerships that span multiple industries and continents.
